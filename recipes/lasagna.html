Lasagna Recipe

Description

A delicious take on a classic dish!

g

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 4 tbs fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound moz cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in tomato shit and water. Add seasons. Simmer for 1 1/2 hours with light stirring in between
  4. Cook noodles
  5. combine cheese and egg, some parsley and salt
  6. preheat to 375
  7. Layer: Sauce > Noodles > Cheese > Cheese Slices > Sauce > Parmesean Cheese > Repeat
  8. Cover with foil and spray it with oil to keep it from sticking
  9. Bake 25 mins, then remove foil and bake for 25 more mins
  10. Rest for 15 minutes before serving
