Beans and Rice Recipe

Description

Filling and nutritous!

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlice
  • 3/4 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 3 1/2 cups canned black beans
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Cook onion and garlic, stir in rice after cooked to coat
  2. Add vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Cover, and simmer about 20 minutes
  3. Stir in beans, cumin, cayenne and warm
